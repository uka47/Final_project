My perception of concept art came from video games and movies, and gradually I realized that it was not only limited to video games and movies. I studied a lot of fine art before I entered UCSD. I was looking forward to the future of computers and AI, but also full of aspirations for art. Great art is intergenerational and ahead of its time, and this is what the development of computers brings to me. I believe that anything is possible.


I love video games and movies, and any story creation is something I want to work on. I will probably study the concept design of video games and movies in more depth after I graduate from UCSD. I want to have more opportunities to study creative content and have the opportunity to learn from great teams and peers.
